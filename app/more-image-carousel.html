These 13 photos capture the best the country has to offer.

Every once in a while it’s important to remember just how lucky we are to live in a country like Canada. Not only is it a place where anyone can live life how they’d like, but it’s a gorgeous piece of land too. In our Spring-Summer issue, we asked readers to send in photos that best describe this country and people sure delivered. Investors Group received about 1000 entries, but only 13 photos – all of which will appear in the company’s 2015 calendar – could be chosen.  The winning shots are below.

Didn’t get a chance to enter this year? No problem! The contest runs annually, so start thinking about 2016’s calendar. Visit the “already a client” section on investorsgroup.com for more information.
